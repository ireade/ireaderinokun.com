Ire Aderinokun

I’m a frontend developer and user interface designer from Lagos, Nigeria.

I’m the Cofounder, COO, and VP Engineering of BuyCoins (YC S18), a cryptocurrency exchange for Africa. We also built Sendcash, a cryptocurrency-powered remittance platform to easily send money to countries in Africa from around the world.

I speak at conferences worldwide, and organise Frontstack, a conference for frontend engineering in Nigeria. I've also written over a hundred technical articles on my blog, bitsofcode.

I am also one of the founding members of the Feminist Coalition, a group of women with a mission to champion equality for women in Nigerian society.

  • Udacity Scholarship for Nigerian Women - A full scholarship for selected Nigerian women to complete a Udacity Nanodegree of their choosing.
